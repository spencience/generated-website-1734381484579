The Autonomous Vehicle Decision

An autonomous vehicle is approaching an unavoidable accident scenario. It must choose between:

  • Swerving left, which would impact a group of elderly pedestrians
  • Continuing straight, which would hit a school bus full of children
  • Swerving right, which would sacrifice its single passenger

What would you choose?
