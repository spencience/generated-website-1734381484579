Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Hello Moral, we believe that privacy is a fundamental human right and ethical imperative. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

  • Basic account information (name, email)
  • Learning progress and preferences
  • Discussion contributions and interactions
  • Usage analytics to improve our services

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Facilitate meaningful discussions
  • Improve our educational content
  • Send relevant updates and notifications

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction or deletion
  • Opt-out of communications
  • Export your learning history

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us or email our Privacy Officer at privacy@hellomoral.com
