Usage Guidelines

By using Hello Moral, you agree to engage in thoughtful, respectful discussions about ethics and moral philosophy. Our platform is designed to foster meaningful dialogue and learning about moral principles and their practical applications.

Code of Conduct

  • Respect diverse viewpoints and perspectives
  • Engage in good-faith discussions
  • Support claims with reasoned arguments
  • Avoid personal attacks or hostile behavior
  • Maintain intellectual honesty

Content Policy

Content shared on Hello Moral should be:

  • Related to ethics, philosophy, or moral reasoning
  • Original or properly attributed
  • Free from harmful or discriminatory content
  • Constructive and conducive to learning
